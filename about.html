Our Story

From vision to reality: How FloCare was born

2024

The Inspiration

Mr. Victor noticed his wife and female friends struggling with inadequate menstrual products, especially during heavy flow days. The search for comfortable, reliable protection began.

2025

FloCare is Born

Together with Mrs. Victor and Miss Uyo, the trio conducted extensive research with women across different age groups to understand their specific needs and challenges.

Present

Making a Difference

Today, FloCare provides premium menstrual solutions specifically designed for women with heavy flow, PCOS, fibroids, and postpartum recovery needs.

Our Mission

To revolutionize menstrual care by providing products that offer superior comfort, reliability, and protection specifically designed for women's unique needs.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted menstrual care brand, breaking taboos and empowering women to live confidently every day of their cycle.

Our Promise

100% comfort, 100% protection, 100% confidence. We stand by our products and the women who trust them.
