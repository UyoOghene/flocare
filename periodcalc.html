Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

Knowledge about your cycle helps you prepare and choose the right protection

Menstrual Phase

Days 1-5: Your period. The uterus sheds its lining. This is when you need reliable protection like FloCare pads.

Follicular Phase

Days 1-13: Your body prepares for ovulation. Estrogen rises, and follicles in the ovaries mature.

Ovulation

Day 14: An egg is released from the ovary. This is your most fertile day.

Luteal Phase

Days 15-28: The body prepares for a potential pregnancy. If no pregnancy occurs, PMS symptoms may appear.
